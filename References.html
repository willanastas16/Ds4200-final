References

The Polaris Data Insights Report and Global Estimates of Modern Slavery will provide key contextual evidence and data to support our analysis of the CTDC dataset. The Polaris report offers insights into trafficking trends like recruitment methods and victim demographics which will be useful for comparing patterns from the CTDC dataset. The ILO’s estimates of forced labour and forced marriage will help to contextualize our findings and understand how socio-demographic factors and exploitation types are reflected in both cases. These references serve to strengthen our analysis through validation and grounding our findings in professional research.
